What is VIP?

Value-averaging investment plan (VIP) is a method of investing in the market that tries to lower the cost of purchase of units more effectively than a regular systematic investment plan. In this method, the amount of money invested on a monthly basis is flexible and varies within a range of minimum and maximum. In contrast, the amount of money invested using SIP is constant every month.

How to invest?

  • VIP is an investment strategy that involves making regular contributions to a portfolio over time.
  • Invest more when the price or portfolio value falls.
  • Invest less when the price or portfolio value rises.
  • Note:VIP is suited for investors who can tolerate investing different sums of money each month.

Example

IF monthly installment is 500 and expected yearly returns % is 12% then monthly rate is 1%. 500 should grow 1% in the 1st month. If the portfolio value is 510 in the 1st month, current value is higher than expected, So invest 495 which is monthly installment(500)-differece(5) where -5 is expected value - current value.
